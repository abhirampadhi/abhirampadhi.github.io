About Me

Hello! I'm Abhiram Padhi, a passionate Full Stack Developer with over 11 years of professional experience. My expertise spans across backend technologies like .NET Core, .NET Framework, and cutting-edge front-end frameworks like Angular.

I specialize in building scalable, high-performance applications that solve real-world problems. My approach focuses on:

  • Implementing SOLID principles and design patterns
  • Architecting microservices and cloud-native applications
  • Establishing CI/CD pipelines for continuous deployment
  • Ensuring code quality through TDD and BDD practices

I thrive in collaborative environments where I can mentor junior developers, contribute to architectural decisions, and drive innovation through technology. I'm continuously learning and staying updated with industry trends.
